Modern Design Solutions

Interior Design Doesn't Have to Be Expensive or Complicated! It Should Be Smart & User Friendly AAKRITI SPACE DESIGNS shapes your dream home into reality. We create fusion designs inspired from world cultures.

AAKRITI SPACE DESIGNS is young and dynamic team for residential and commercial interior designs. One of the best interior designers in Mumbai operating from Kandivali, provides turnkey one-stop interior design solutions in Kandivali, Malad, Borivali, Andheri, Vile Parle, Santacruz, Juhu, as per clients requirements. We also have a strong foothold in Pune, Nashik, Bengaluru and Ahemadabad with core team to build and supervise projects like, residential apartments, commercial offices, Cafes, Hotels and restaurants, Fitness centres, Salons, retail stores, supermarkets with end to end solution from design to build.

With years of experience and highly skilled set of carpenters, electrician, civil, painters and onsite supervisors we ensure your interiors are not only designed but also delivered as per your requirement, well within a fair budget and time frame. AAKRITI SPACE DESIGN is associated with the best brands in hardware and fittings hence promises quality and post-completion certificate with service warranties.
